Recipe for Pizza:

Recipe:

The recipe will need flour, tomato sauce, pepporoni, black olives, and cheese. The recipe will approximently take 1 hour-1:30 Hour and a half to complete.

Ingredient list:

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepporoni
  • Black Olive

Steps:

  1. Add water to flour and knead dough
  2. Spread dough out to pizza shape
  3. Apply tomato sauce to dough and cheese on top
  4. Apply pepporoni on top of cheese
  5. Bake in oven for 25 - 30 Minutes
  6. After done baking, cut into 8 even slices
