Chicken Strip Recipe:

Recipe Content:

This recipe contains chicken, flour, and multiple types of seasoning. The recipe will take about 1 hour to complete and be ready.

Ingredient List:

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cover chicken with seasoning
  2. Cover chicken with flour
  3. Cook in oil for 25 minutes total
  4. Should be ready to eat
