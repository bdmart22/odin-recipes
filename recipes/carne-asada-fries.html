Carne Asada Fries

Recipe Description:

This recipe is quick and easy and should take about 40 minutes.

It will serve two people for one serving.

Recipe:

  • French Fries
  • Roast Beef
  • Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream

Steps to complete:

  1. Cut meat into small cubes and cook for 15 minutes and make sure to season.
  2. Take french fries and cook in oil.
  3. Combine meat and fries and top with guacamole, cheese, and sour cream.
